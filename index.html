About Me

I am a graduate student at Northeastern University, currently pursuing my Masters’ in Information Systems. I graduated from Atharva College of Engineering affiliated to Mumbai University in May’15. I completed my Bachelors in the field of Information Technology. After my graduation, I worked at IGATE Global Solutions for 4 months as a Software Engineer during which I was trained in .NET technology and worked on a project based on .NET MVC framework. Later on, I freelanced as a web developer. I am well versed in programming languages like C, C++, C#, Java, Visual Basic, T-SQL and in web technologies like HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, Bootstrap, AngularJS, Express.js, Node.js. I have a first-hand experience of working on database management systems like Microsoft SQL server, Oracle, MongoDB. I wish to work as full stack developer after I graduate from Northeastern University. After mastering the technical skills, I also want to develop and excel in managerial aspects of software project management while working towards my ultimate professional goal.
